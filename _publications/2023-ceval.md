**C-Eval: A Multi-Level Multi-Discipline Chinese Evaluation Suite for Foundation Models**

**Authors**: Yuzhen Huang, Yuzhuo Bai, Zhihao Zhu, Junlei Zhang, Jinghan Zhang, Tangjun Su, Junteng Liu, Chuancheng Lv, Yikai Zhang, Jiayi Lei, Yao Fu, Maosong Sun, Junxian He

**Venue**: Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS), 2023

**Abstract**: We present C-Eval, a comprehensive evaluation suite for foundation models in Chinese language. C-Eval covers 52 disciplines ranging from humanities to science and technology, across four difficulty levels from middle school to professional level, providing a thorough assessment of model capabilities in reasoning, knowledge, and problem-solving.
