**SynLogic: Synthesizing Verifiable Reasoning Data at Scale for Learning Logical Reasoning and Beyond**

**Authors**: Junteng Liu, Yuanxiang Fan, Zhuo Jiang, Han Ding, Yongyi Hu, Chi Zhang, Yiqi Shi, Shitong Weng, Aili Chen, Shiqi Chen, Yunan Huang, Mozhi Zhang, Pengyu Zhao, Junjie Yan, Junxian He

**Venue**: Arxiv, 2025

**Abstract**: This paper presents SynLogic, a framework for synthesizing verifiable reasoning data at scale for learning logical reasoning and beyond. The approach generates high-quality reasoning data that can be automatically verified, enabling efficient training of models for logical reasoning tasks.
