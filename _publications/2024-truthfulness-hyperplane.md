**On the Universal Truthfulness Hyperplane Inside LLMs**

**Authors**: Junteng Liu, Shiqi Chen, Yu Cheng, Junxian He

**Venue**: Proceedings of the 2024 Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing (EMNLP), 2024

**Abstract**: In this paper, we discover a universal truthfulness hyperplane inside large language models (LLMs) that can effectively detect untruthful statements across different tasks and model sizes. Our findings provide insights into the internal mechanisms of LLMs regarding truthfulness and suggest a simple yet effective approach for hallucination detection.
