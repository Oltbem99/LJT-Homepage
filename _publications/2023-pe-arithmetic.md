**Composing Parameter-Efficient Modules with Arithmetic Operations**

**Authors**: Jinghan Zhang, Shiqi Chen, Junteng Liu, Junxian He

**Venue**: Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS), 2023

**Abstract**: We investigate the composition of parameter-efficient modules (PEMs) using arithmetic operations. Our approach enables flexible model adaptation by combining different PEMs through simple mathematical operations, achieving competitive performance across various tasks with minimal parameter overhead.
