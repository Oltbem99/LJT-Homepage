**On the Perception Bottleneck of VLMs for Chart Understanding**

**Authors**: Junteng Liu, Weihao Zeng, Xiwen Zhang, Yijun Wang, Zifei Shan, Junxian He

**Venue**: Arxiv, 2025

**Abstract**: This paper investigates the perception bottleneck of vision-language models (VLMs) for chart understanding tasks. We identify key limitations in how current VLMs process visual chart data and propose approaches to improve their visual reasoning capabilities.
